Gem Hunter


You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four gem types below. By clicking on a gem, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. If your total score goes beyond the random number, you will lose the game.

The value of each gem is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each gem.
