Gem Hunter


You have been be given an allowable quota value of gems to mine.

There are four gem types below. By clicking on a gem, you will add a specific amount of gem value to your total score. Don't be greedy!

You win the game by matching your value with the allowed quota. If your total score goes beyond it, you will lose the game.

The value of each gem is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of the gems will change.
